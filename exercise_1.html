Last Opinions

El Paso
Talavera de La Reina, Spain
21/09/2018

With a great selection of innovative american food and comedy, El Paso presents itself as one of the most attractive restaurants in the city. This is not only about its food, but of the "Comedy Fridays", where famous spanish comedians ease the eating of the fortunate customers, who will have a great meal and an unforgetable experience.

Tagliatella
Alcorcón, Spain
06/09/2018

Located inside a greatly decorated environment, La Tagliatella will make its customers rejoice with an incredible amount of plates greatly selected among the best typical italian food. Their secret: the high quality ingredients, the highly skilled cooks and the food amount (It won't dissappoint you!).

Ruiz de Luna
Talavera de La Reina, Spain
12/06/2018

A creative cuisine restaurant in the city of ceramic. An intensive study of the land's highest quality products, Ruiz de Luna does not forget its roots. The high level craftsmanship in the creation of their dishes presents a great love for tradition, definitely a meal that must be experienced by all visitors of the city.
